Who is TxT?

TxT, or Tomorrow by Together, is a K-pop group with millions of global fans. They have a fanbase called "Moa" which stands for "Moments of alwaysness." There are 5 members, Soobin (who is the leader), Yeonjun, Taehyun, Beumgyu, and Huening Kai. They are under the company BigHit, which is the same company BTS is under.
